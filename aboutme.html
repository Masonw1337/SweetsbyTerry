Why Me?

Let me tell you!

I’ve always loved baking. I started baking mini apple pies, cheesecakes, brownies, and cookies. I’d bake all day on Sunday and take the baked goods to work with me on Monday. Within 2 hours, I’d be sold out. I started taking orders from coworkers for the upcoming week. I was so happy to do what I really loved, and I made a little money too! A friend of mine had a birthday coming up, and she loved the Minions! I decided to take my first crack at making a character cake. That’s where the love of making custom cakes started. I love to find out what people like. Their hobbies, what they do for fun, and then I make a special cake for them. I hope I can continue making cakes and making people smile for years to come!
